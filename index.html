Zefan Yang is a 3rd-year master's student at Shenzhen University from China. He obatined a B.Eng. degree in Biomedical Engineering at Shenzhen University in 2020. His research interest lies in artificial intelligence for medical image analysis, with a particular focus on annotation-efficient learning. He is also exploring robust and interpretable artificial intelligence for medical and healthcare applications.
